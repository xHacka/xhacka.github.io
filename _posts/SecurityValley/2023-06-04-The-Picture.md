## Solution

We can use [zsteg](https://github.com/zed-0xff/zsteg) to detect stegano-hidden data
```sh
└─$ zsteg challenge.png                                                   
b1,r,lsb,xy         .. text: "vwpR&?|"
b1,rgb,lsb,xy       .. text: "SecVal{REDACTED}"
b1,rgba,msb,xy      .. file: OpenPGP Public Key
```
